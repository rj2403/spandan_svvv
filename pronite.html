Band

"Dhaiwat" is a 6-piece Band taking experimental music to a whole new level. Although they do not restrict themselves to a defined genre, they show their musical brilliance in Indian classical, folk, fusion and progressive/alternative rock. Intending to offer good and seasoned music to the people, Dhaiwat explores and inspirits the beauty of classical and folk music blending it with rock styles which makes the band stand out from the rest. Their innovative experiments with music win hearts wherever they go.

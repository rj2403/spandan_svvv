Fireless Cooking

Fireless Cooking Competition provides the students a platform to showcase their skills in cooking. The participants will have to prepare dishes without putting fire. The dishes will be judged on the basis of taste and decoration of the servings.

Details:
● In this round the teams will have to prepare a dish or drink within 45 mins.
● Participants should not bring any pre-cooked items.
● Participants are supposed to display a chart having the name, ingredients.
● All the items required for cooking should be brought by the participants themselves.
● Only working table will be provided.
● Dishes should be purely vegetarian.
● Participants should not bring any cut, chopped or grated raw materials. They should process it on the spot within allotted time.
● The evaluation will be based on the taste of the dish, nutritional level, cleanliness and presentation.
